SMS service

With ChaloBEST, you can send queries and receive information about bus stops, bus numbers and routing on BEST.
SMS to 96195.24420 (*)

(*) Normal SMS charges applicable as per carrier rate and plan.

 

ChaloBEST SMS is in public beta, and your feedback is most welcome, click here to contact.

➥ Mahim to churchgate

Routes between bus stops / areas

To know which routes to take between any bus stop or area, send the names separated by "to".

Example: "Colaba depot to worli"
(or "malad w to bandra talkies", "vikhroli e to ghatkopar w", etc.)

➥ Mahim

All busses + frequencies per stop

To know the routes and frequencies at any bus stop, send the name.

Example: "mahim"
(or "borivali w", "bhandup", etc.)

➥ 21Ltd

Bus route & frequency

To know the route and frequency of any bus, send the bus number and letter.

Example: "21"
